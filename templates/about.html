About

Mission

The Al-Imdaad Society is a non-profit humanitarian organisation (NGO) registered in Telangana State of India, dedicated to providing humanitarian services in crisis and non-crisis situations to most needy widows, orphans and destitute.

Our Team

The Team of the Al-Imdaad Society dedicated to serve humanity with the skills and expertise that they are blessed with. The team comprises of Engineers, Doctors, Advocates, IT Specialists, Businessmen and other voluntary workers.

Who We Are

We are a Small Team working for over 12 years for the financial development of Muslim poor and needy people in the society. We are passionate about what we do and constantly strive to achieve excellence.

What We Do

We work for the uplifment of Muslim poor and needy people by providing financial assistance in marriages, education, treatment aid and monthly pension for the older and widow women in the society.

How We do

We beleive in Collective System of Zakath, i.e we collect donations(zakath/Sadqa) throught the year mostly in the month of Ramazan from wealthy people and spend among the Muslim poor which allows us to perform our operations efficiently.

Where We do

We are currently working in and around the Town of Narayanpet, Telangana State, India. But we strongly beleive that with your donations and assistance we can expand our services in a much better way.
