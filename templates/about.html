About

Mission

The Al-Imdaad Society is a non-profit humanitarian organisation (NGO) registered in Telangana State of India, started with the main aim to establish Collective System of Zakath and to distribute it round the year.

Why We're Unique

The amount collected is purely distributed to the needy without any penny going towards the societyâ€™s expenditures.

History

On the auspicious occasion of Ramazan-ul-Mubarak in October 2005, society was established under the guidance and chairmanship of Alhaj Ahmed Meeran Mohiuddin Taj, Retired Engineer-in-Chief (R&B Dept.). Alhamdulillah this work has been going on since last 12 years. Mr. Abdul Kareem Taj, a teacher by occupation dedicated his time and has been acting as a Zakath Distributor as well as the Treasurer of the society.

Our Team

The Team of the Al-Imdaad Society dedicated to serve humanity with the skills and expertise that they are blessed with. The team comprises of Engineers, Doctors, Advocates, IT Specialists, Businessmen and other voluntary workers.

What We Do

We provide financial assistance for poor Muslim widows, marriage of poor Muslim daughters, loan payments, educational assistance for poor Muslim families including providing basic Islamic knowledge, medical assistance and employment assistance with emphasis on self-employment of girls.

How We Do

We beleive in Collective System of Zakath, i.e the total collected zakath amount from various honorable persons in Ramazan-ul-Mubarak is distributed round the year among 300+ beneficiaries as a monthly allowance of INR.300 (Indian Rupee) for basic food expenses. Apart from this, INR.6000 to INR.10000 is also allotted for Marriages, Medical Treatments, Educational Assistance and Loan Repayment for eligible poor persons.

Where We Do

We are currently serving in and around the Town of Narayanpet, Telangana State, India. But we strongly beleive that with your continuous donations and assistance we can expand our services in a much greater way.
