Mission

The Al-Imdaad Society is a non-profit humanitarian organisation (NGO) registered in Telangana State of India, started with the main aim to establish Collective System of Zakath and to distribute it round the year.

History

On the auspicious occasion of Ramazan-ul-Mubarak in October 2005, society was established under the guidance and chairmanship of Alhaj Ahmed Meeran Mohiuddin Taj, Retired Engineer-in-Chief (RandB). Alhamdulillah this work has been going on since last 12 years. Mr. Abdul Kareem Taj, a teacher by occupation has dedicated his time and has been acting as a Zakath Distributor as well as the Treasurer of the society.
