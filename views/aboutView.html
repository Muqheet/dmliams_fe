Who We Are

We are a Small Team working for over 10 years for thefinancial development of Muslim poor and needy people in theSociety. We are passionate about what we do and strive to achieve excellence.

What We Do

We work for the uplifment of Muslim poor and needy people byproviding financial assistance in marriages, education, treatmentaid and pension for the older and widow women.

How We do

We beleive in Collective System of Zakath, i.e we collect donations(zakath) throught the year mostly in the month of Ramazan from the wealthy people and spend among the needs of Muslim poor people.
